What we do

Dog walking£8, 30mins

This is the perfect choice for dogs who aren't a fan of other dogs or just prefer to be walked alone. This is half an hours walk which can be tracked and timed, messages as we set off/return and a quick wash so your pet returns home looking and smelling as fresh as when they set off.

Drop-in visits£8, 30mins

This visit can include various things from playing with the young energetic dogs, to a snooze and a stroke for the more relaxed dogs. I can also feed and administer medicine and I am happy to brush your dogs to help with any matting and messy hair.

Group dog walks£12, 1hr

This walk is perfect for high energy dogs. This walk will be in a park or a safe place and dogs will be allowed off the lead (with permission) to have a good run. This walk will also include a quick wash at the end to make sure your dog returns looking and smelling as good as when they left.

Watch a walk

Safety is key for me so every walk is GPS tracked, and you get a text when leave and when we get back home. Whether your dog can go off lead or not, they'll get a full walk and play. Finally, every dog gets a quick wash so they return home smelling fresh!

I'm DBS checked and available for walks in Wimblington, Chatteris, Doddington, March and wider Cambridgeshire.

Meet my dog, Coco

Let me introduce you to Coco!

She's a 10 year old rescue Rottweiler that's a bit nervous of everything! We've had her around 6/7 months now in that time we've had to remove a toe so she now has an alien foot!

If you ever see us out on a walk she'll definitely bark but please do say hello! She'll be wearing a muzzle just in case but she really just wants a stroke!

Unfortunately she wasn't socialized when she was younger so she's very barky at everyone and everything that moves but if the barking doesn't scare you she'll happily accept your cuddles!
